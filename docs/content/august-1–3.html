  • August 1-3

    Denver, Colorado

    DrupalCamp Colorado is an annual gathering of people learning and discussing the open-source content management system, Drupal. Sessions will cover topics ranging from the basic to the more complex, with plenty in between. Join us in beautiful Denver, Colorado to share, learn, collaborate, and get involved in the community!

  • Ken Rickard, Our 2nd Keynote!

    Palantir's Director of Development and Professional Services

    Ken Rickard is the Director of Development and Professional Services at Chicago firm Palantir.net, and oversees the planning, development and deployment of Drupal and web applications for clients like Public Radio International, Foreign Affairs, the University of Minnesota, and the Field Museum.

  • Michael Meyers is Keynoting!

    Acquia's VP of Large Scale Drupal

    We're thrilled to announce that Michael Myers of Acquia will come and keynote on Large Scale Drupal. Meyers is an Emmy-nominated technologist and entrepreneur. As CTO of The Clarity Digital Group, which acquired NowPublic in 2009, Michael led Examiner.com’s migration to open source technology, launching the first Drupal-based top-100 Internet property.
